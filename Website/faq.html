FAQ

  • Q. How old do I need to be to join the team?
    A. The minimum age is 13 years. Admission below 13 years are negotiatied on a case by case basis.
  • Q. Can I join the team if I'm not homeschooled?
    A. Children who are part of a school without an active VEX Robotics team, may be eligable for membership.
  • Q. When and where does the team meet?
    A. The team meets at the Massey School of Engineering and Advanced Technology in Albany. We meet on Thursday from 4-6pm.
  • Q. What is the price of membership?
    A. The club offers a 3 month trial period, after this the annual subscription is $100. For familes with more than 1 member the fee is $100 for the first child, then $50 for subsequent children.
